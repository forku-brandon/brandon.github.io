Working Process

Our Working Process - How We Work For Our Customers

Contact Us
1.

Initiation

In the earliest stage, after the client contacts us for the first time, we process the incoming request. Afterward, we assign them a Project Manager who will accompany the project through the development process, until its successful completion.

2.

Vision

Here our Project Manager constructs an understanding of what the product is made for, what problems it solves, its core functionality, and what client's business and future users expect. We do that through the in-depth analysis of the business. It can be conducted remotely or on-site if necessary. When the analysis is complete, we form an initial vision of the project to offer the most suitable decisions based on the collected data and tech expertise.

3.

Functionality

Finally, after we have established a vision, we begin to figure out functional requirements for the project. At this stage, we never plan the project entirely, as it will inevitably change in the process of development. Thatâ€™s why we figure out the core functionality of the project and begin the development when clients are ready to start. By following this approach, we can determine the best solutions and think of the best ways to implement them.

4.

Development

Agile methodology means ceaseless work and improvement of the product. We put the continuous business analysis in the foundation of project development to simplify the process of making changes and looking for the best solutions, based on what we have and how it works.
